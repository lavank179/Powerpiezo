Power Calculation in Area - For a Piezo's installed tile.

# For Ideal Condition : :
Avg. Voltage of piezo.

= 2V
1 Tile of Square meter contains 12x12
Piezo's Connected in series.

 = 144 x 2 => 288V

Power in Watts per sec.

= V x I                                  where I = current , V = voltage

= 288 x 25 x 10^-6              [i.e I = 25 micro Amp].

= 7200 micro Watt /sec        or 0.0072 W/sec.
Power for 1 Hour

 = 0.0072 x 60 x 60

= 25.92 W/ per hour.
Power for 1 day (16 Hours) Working.

 = 25.92 x 16

= 414.72 W/day.
## Assume 100 Sq. meter area fixed with 100 tiles.

 ....
Total power generated for 1 day.

 = 414.72 x 100

= 41,472 W/day.
:: Unit for 1 day

 = 41,472 / 1000                   1 Unit = 1000 W.

= 41.472 Units / day.
Estimated power for 1 day in 100 Sq.ft Area

~= 41 Units / day.
For Non-ideal Conditions : :

 ......
Power generated by 144 Piezo's is

= 200 x 25 x 10 ^-6 W.

= 0.005 W / sec.
For 1 day

= 288 W / day.
For 100 Sq. feet Area , the total is

= 28.8 Units / day.

For Non-Ideal Condition - min. Power

29 Units / day. per 100 Sq.meter.

For Ideal Condition - max. Power

41 Units / day. per 100 Sq.meter.
